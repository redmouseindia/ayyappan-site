About Lord Ayyappan

Lord Ayyappan, also known as Manikandan, is the son of Lord Shiva and Mohini (Vishnu's female form). He symbolizes unity, devotion, and dharma. His abode, Sabarimala, is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in the world.

Pilgrimage Guide

  • 41-Day Vratham and Fasting
  • Wearing black clothes and mala
  • Irumudi Kettu preparation
  • Sabarimala route details and Pamba guidance
